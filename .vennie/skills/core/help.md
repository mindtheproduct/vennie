# What Can Vennie Do?

Show the user everything Vennie can help with, organised so they can find what they need fast.

## Display Format

Present a clean, categorised overview:

```
## Product Work
- `/prd` — Write a PRD through guided conversation (not a template)
- `/strategy` — Strategic sparring with devil's advocate mode
- `/spec` — Generate technical specs from PRDs or ideas
- `/prioritise` — Score and rank features with RICE, ICE, or custom frameworks
- `/decision` — Capture decisions with context, rationale, and review dates

## Career Development
- `/coach` — Career coaching session with evidence-backed insights
- `/review-prep` — Prepare for performance reviews using your evidence trail
- `/1on1` — Prepare for and capture takeaways from 1:1 meetings
- `/negotiate` — Prepare for salary, title, and scope negotiations
- `/wins` — Quick weekly wins capture (2-3 minutes)
- `/brag` — Compile evidence into formatted brag sheets
- `/resume` — Generate tailored resumes from your evidence
- `/cover-letter` — Write authentic cover letters for specific roles
- `/interview-prep` — Company research and story mapping for interviews

## Brand Building
- `/linkedin` — Write posts, review your profile, plan content strategy
- `/write` — Write newsletters, blog posts, articles in your voice
- `/talk` — Prepare conference talks from idea to CFP submission

## Intelligence
- `/news` — Today's AI signal — the one thing worth knowing
- `/changelog` — Tool updates that matter for your workflow
- `/landscape` — Competitive landscape analysis

## System & Setup
- `/persona` — Manage AI personas (activate, create, install)
- `/voice` — Train Vennie to write in your voice
- `/status` — Dashboard of everything — projects, goals, decisions, evidence
- `/connect` — Set up tool integrations (Jira, Linear, Slack, GitHub, etc.)
- `/daily-plan` — Start your day with a focused plan
- `/weekly-review` — End the week with clarity and set up next week
- `/quarterly-review` — Big picture review and goal setting
```

## Getting Started Tips

If the user seems new, add:

```
## Getting the Most Out of Vennie

**Start here:**
1. `/daily-plan` — try it tomorrow morning. Takes 2 minutes.
2. `/wins` — capture this week's wins. Builds your evidence trail.
3. `/decision` next time you make a product call. Your future self will thank you.

**Level up:**
- `/voice train` — teach Vennie your writing style
- `/persona list` — try a persona for a different perspective
- `/connect` — hook up your tools for richer context

**The power move:**
Evidence compounds. Every win you capture, every decision you log, every coaching session — 
it all builds a picture of your career that's incredibly valuable for reviews, negotiations, 
and job searches. The best time to start was months ago. The second best time is now.
```
